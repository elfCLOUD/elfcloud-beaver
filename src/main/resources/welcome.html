What is the elfcloud.fi Beaver?

elfcloud.fi Beaver is a client program which allows you to connect to elfcloud.fi secure storage services.

What can I do with the elfcloud.fi Beaver?

elfcloud.fi Beaver allows you to securely store all your data to elfcloud.fi service. Your data will be encrypted locally using your encryption keys. Encryption keys are never sent to elfcloud.fi.

You can easily browse all the files and folders you have stored to elfcloud.fi service as well as store new content and retrieve the content that you have already stored.

What I need to do in order to use elfcloud.fi services?

First you need to register an elfcloud.fi account. This is an quick and easy task and you can do it at my.elfcloud.fi. Select the account type which suits your needs and follow the instructions.

After you have registered an account and agreed the End User License Agreement term, you can continue by logging in to my.elfcloud.fi with the credentials you provided during the registration process.

You are provided with limited amount of free storage space. You can increase available storage amount by adding a subscription to your account. This is quick process and can be completed using the web-interface at my.elfcloud.fi.
